Last updated: April 17, 2025 · Effective: April 17, 2025

PayPe Technologies Pvt. Ltd. offers a fair and transparent refund policy in compliance with the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020 and applicable Indian consumer protection laws.

1. Credit Purchases

Credits purchased on the PayPe Technologies platform are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:

  • Refund request is submitted within 7 days of purchase
  • Fewer than 5 credits have been consumed from the purchased bundle
  • The request is submitted via email to admin@paype.co.in with your order/payment ID

2. Subscription Plans

  • Monthly subscription fees are non-refundable once the billing cycle has commenced
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No partial refunds are issued for unused days within a billing period
  • Annual plan subscribers may request a pro-rata refund within 14 days of purchase if fewer than 10 builds have been used

If credits are deducted due to a confirmed platform error or technical failure on our end (e.g., a build fails due to server error), those credits will be fully restored to your account within 48 hours. To report a technical failure, email itsupport@paype.co.in with details.

Credits purchased during promotional or discounted sale periods (such as limited-time 50% off offers) are eligible for refunds under the same 7-day / 5-credit policy stated above. The refund amount will be the discounted price actually paid, not the original price.
